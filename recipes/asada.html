Carne Asada

*Image and recipe is from Pinch and Swirl

Carne Asada is an amazing way to prepare steak. Not only it is fantastic by itself you can also use it in many other dishes such as tacos, enchiladas, and more. The marinade of the steak is perfect as many of the ingredients serve as tenderizing agents and bring out the best flavor of the cut of steak.

    Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds skirt steak trimmed of excess fat
  • 1 jalape√±o seeded and minced
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves chopped
  • juice of 1 orange
  • juice of 1 lime
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp. apple cider vinegar
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper

    Steps:

  1. In a bowl whisk together all marinade ingredients, and then afterward place the steak inside and latter. Marinade for atleast 1 hour.
  2. Grill on medium-high heat for 7 to 10 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  3. Let rest for 7 minutes and slice across the grain before serving
