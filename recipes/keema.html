Keema

*Image and recipe is from Eat.Drink.Pure

Keema is a Pakistani dish made from minced meat which is cooked with various spices. From the description it seems like a very basic dish and it is.

    Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp. oil
  • 2 lb. ground beef
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 2 tsp. red chili powder
  • 2 tsp. coriander powder
  • 1 tsp. garlic paste
  • 1 tsp. ginger paste
  • 1 tsp. turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp. cumin powder
  • 1 chopped medium tomato
  • 1 chopped green chili pepper
  • 1/2 diced medium onion

    Steps:

  1. In medium sized pot fry onions on medium high heat until they are golden brown
  2. Add ginger and garlic and stir fry for 30 seconds
  3. Add beef and combine. Break up beef as you stir because it will clump
  4. Once beef is combined, add spices and stir
  5. Add tomatoes and green chili, lower heat and cover for 15 to 20 minutes
  6. Once tomatoes have softened. Increase heat to high and stir mixture until water dries
